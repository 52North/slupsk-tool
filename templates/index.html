Creating Interfaces - Słupsk

Eat locally and healthily! Welcome to the food system in Słupsk!

We all love food. We make sure our children eat healthily. We are paying more and more attention to what we eat and what we buy. What we eat affects not only us, but also the environment and our immediate surroundings. With this application, we will try to provide you with information about food in Słupsk. Moreover, by answering some questions in this application you can help us to better understand the food nexus. Using the map you can:  

  • check what children eat in kindergartens in Słupsk, rate the meals and see how other people rate them, find out where the products for the meals come from,
  • get to know local food shops and their products,
  • get to know local producers and their products,
  • share what you eat and where you buy food with others,
  • help in understanding the environmental impact of what you eat and what children eat in kindergartens.

The application was developed in cooperation with the Słupsk City and on the basis of the collected data on the food needs of the inhabitants of Słupsk. We are currently testing the first version of the application. Share with us your opinion about the application by clicking on the link with a few questions!
If you got login data by the project team, you can login here to use additional features of the application.

Discover food close to you!

What can you find out? What can you do?
You want to buy locally, but don't know where to buy food from local farmers? You will find them on this map. You can also help build a map of local producers by adding them to the map yourself! If you already buy from this producer you can evaluate the products he/she sells. Leave your comment and discuss with others about your favourite producers and food products! If you produce food yourself and want to leave more detailed data about your products - contact us!
